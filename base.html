I am now experienced in HTML, CSS, Javascript, mySQL, JQuery, Java and Spring. I am also experienced in Git, working remote with diverse types of teams, and mobile to desktop responsive design. Today I enjoy spending time with my cohort, solving challenging problems, creating unique features for unique designs, and studying new technology to add to my toolbox.
